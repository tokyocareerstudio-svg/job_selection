SECTION 01
SPI 영어검사란?
SPI英語検査の概要

SPI 영어검사(ENG)는 테스트센터에서만 실시되는 선택 과목입니다. 기업이 영어 능력을 평가하고 싶을 때 언어·비언어에 추가로 부과하며, 제한시간은 약 20분입니다. 종합상사, 외자계, 글로벌 메이커 등에서 자주 출제됩니다.

출제 유형 4가지
① 동의어 (Synonyms) — 밑줄 친 단어와 가장 가까운 뜻의 선택지 고르기
② 반의어 (Antonyms) — 밑줄 친 단어와 반대 뜻의 선택지 고르기
③ 빈칸보충 (Blank Fill) — 문맥에 맞는 단어·숙어를 빈칸에 채우기
④ 장문독해 (Reading) — 영어 지문을 읽고 내용 일치/불일치 판별
주의사항
SPI 영어는 TOEIC과 달리 어휘력 비중이 매우 높습니다. 동의어·반의어만으로 절반 가까이 출제되므로, 비즈니스 영어 어휘를 집중 암기하는 것이 가장 효율적인 대책입니다. 독해는 TOEIC 600점 수준이면 충분합니다.

SECTION 03
유형별 예제 20문
タイプ別 例題20問

각 유형의 전형적인 출제 패턴을 익히세요. 풀이 버튼을 클릭하면 해설이 나옵니다.

EX-01 BASIC
The CEO made an astute decision.
A. clever　B. hasty　C. reckless　D. generous　E. modest
ANSWER
정답: A. clever astute = 기민한, 영리한 → clever(영리한)가 동의어 hasty(성급한), reckless(무모한), generous(관대한), modest(겸손한)은 의미가 다름
EX-02 BASIC
We need to mitigate the risk.
A. increase　B. evaluate　C. alleviate　D. ignore　E. transfer
ANSWER
정답: C. alleviate mitigate = 완화하다 → alleviate(완화하다)가 동의어 increase(증가시키다), evaluate(평가하다)와 혼동 주의
EX-03 REAL
Her tenacious effort impressed the team.
A. brief　B. persistent　C. feeble　D. sporadic　E. reluctant
ANSWER
정답: B. persistent tenacious = 끈질긴, 집요한 → persistent(지속적인, 끈질긴) sporadic(산발적인)은 반의어에 가까움
EX-04 REAL
The report was comprehensive.
A. brief　B. thorough　C. superficial　D. biased　E. obsolete
ANSWER
정답: B. thorough comprehensive = 포괄적인, 종합적인 → thorough(철저한, 빈틈없는) superficial(표면적인)은 반의어
EX-05 HARD
The policy proved to be detrimental.
A. beneficial　B. neutral　C. harmful　D. temporary　E. innovative
ANSWER
정답: C. harmful detrimental = 해로운, 불리한 → harmful(해로운) beneficial(유익한)은 반의어 — 함정 선택지로 자주 출제
EX-06 BASIC
The instructions were ambiguous.
A. vague　B. explicit　C. lengthy　D. redundant　E. trivial
ANSWER
정답: B. explicit (반의어) ambiguous = 모호한 ↔ explicit = 명확한 vague(모호한)는 동의어이므로 함정
EX-07 BASIC
Sales were sluggish last quarter.
A. slow　B. brisk　C. moderate　D. volatile　E. marginal
ANSWER
정답: B. brisk (반의어) sluggish = 부진한, 느린 ↔ brisk = 활발한, 활기찬 slow(느린)는 동의어이므로 함정
EX-08 REAL
Her approach was pragmatic.
A. practical　B. idealistic　C. cautious　D. flexible　E. creative
ANSWER
정답: B. idealistic (반의어) pragmatic = 실용적인 ↔ idealistic = 이상주의적인 practical(실용적인)은 동의어
EX-09 REAL
The data was abundant.
A. plentiful　B. scarce　C. reliable　D. scattered　E. precise
ANSWER
정답: B. scarce (반의어) abundant = 풍부한 ↔ scarce = 부족한, 희소한 plentiful(풍부한)은 동의어
EX-10 HARD
The CEO is known for being frugal.
A. thrifty　B. extravagant　C. cautious　D. diligent　E. affluent
ANSWER
정답: B. extravagant (반의어) frugal = 검소한 ↔ extravagant = 사치스러운 affluent(부유한)과 혼동 주의 — 부유하다고 반드시 낭비하는 것은 아님
EX-11 BASIC
The company decided to (　　) its operations in Asia.
A. expand　B. expend　C. expose　D. expire　E. export
ANSWER
정답: A. expand expand operations = 사업을 확장하다 expend(소비하다), expose(노출하다), expire(만료되다)와 철자 혼동 주의
EX-12 BASIC
We must (　　) with the new regulations.
A. apply　B. comply　C. supply　D. imply　E. reply
ANSWER
정답: B. comply comply with = ~을 준수하다 (규정 등) apply(적용하다/지원하다)도 with를 취하지만 문맥상 부적절
EX-13 REAL
The manager (　　) the importance of teamwork.
A. emphasized　B. undermined　C. overlooked　D. dismissed　E. neglected
ANSWER
정답: A. emphasized emphasize the importance of = ~의 중요성을 강조하다 undermined(약화시키다), overlooked(간과하다)는 반대 의미
EX-14 REAL
The new policy was (　　) to reduce costs.
A. implemented　B. implicated　C. complicated　D. duplicated　E. simulated
ANSWER
정답: A. implemented implement a policy = 정책을 시행하다 implicated(관련시키다)와 철자 혼동이 최다 오답
EX-15 HARD
The merger is (　　) on regulatory approval.
A. dependent　B. contingent　C. reliant　D. conditional　E. incumbent
ANSWER
정답: B. contingent contingent on = ~에 달려있는, ~을 조건으로 하는 (비즈니스 문맥에서 가장 정확) dependent on도 의미는 통하나, 합병·인수 문맥에서는 contingent이 정형 표현
EX-16 BASIC
다음 지문을 읽고 물음에 답하세요.
Remote work has become a permanent fixture in many industries. A recent survey found that 65% of employees prefer a hybrid model, combining office and home work. Companies that offer flexible arrangements report higher employee satisfaction and lower turnover rates. However, some managers express concerns about maintaining team cohesion and monitoring productivity.
Q. According to the passage, which statement is TRUE?
A. All employees prefer working entirely from home.
B. Flexible work arrangements tend to improve employee retention.
C. Most managers support remote work without reservation.
D. Hybrid models are only popular in the tech industry.
ANSWER
정답: B "lower turnover rates" = 이직률 감소 = 직원 유지율(retention) 향상 A는 "65% prefer hybrid"이므로 "all"은 오답, C는 "some managers express concerns"이므로 오답
EX-17 REAL
다음 지문을 읽고 물음에 답하세요.
Sustainable investing, also known as ESG investing, has gained significant traction in recent years. Investors increasingly consider environmental, social, and governance factors alongside financial returns. Critics argue that ESG criteria can be subjective and may lead to lower returns. Nevertheless, studies have shown that companies with strong ESG practices often outperform their peers in the long term.
Q. What do critics of ESG investing argue?
A. ESG investing always leads to higher returns.
B. ESG standards are universally agreed upon.
C. Evaluating ESG factors can be subjective and may reduce profits.
D. All companies now follow ESG guidelines.
ANSWER
정답: C "subjective and may lead to lower returns" = 주관적이며 수익이 낮아질 수 있다 A는 정반대, B는 "critics argue subjective"이므로 오답
EX-18 REAL
다음 지문을 읽고 물음에 답하세요.
Japan's labor market faces a structural challenge due to its aging population. The working-age population has been declining steadily, prompting companies to adopt automation and hire foreign workers. The government has introduced several visa programs to attract skilled professionals from overseas, particularly in IT and healthcare sectors.
Q. What is NOT mentioned as a response to Japan's labor shortage?
A. Increasing automation in workplaces
B. Recruiting workers from other countries
C. Raising the mandatory retirement age
D. Creating new visa programs for skilled workers
ANSWER
정답: C 지문에서 automation(A), hire foreign workers(B), visa programs(D)는 언급됨 retirement age 인상은 언급되지 않음 — "NOT mentioned" 유형은 소거법으로 풀기
EX-19 HARD
다음 지문을 읽고 물음에 답하세요.
The gig economy has transformed traditional employment patterns. While it offers workers flexibility and autonomy, it also raises concerns about job security and benefits. A study by the International Labour Organization found that gig workers earn, on average, 20% less than their traditionally employed counterparts when benefits are factored in. Proponents argue that the freedom to choose projects compensates for the financial disparity.
Q. What can be inferred from the passage?
A. Gig workers always earn more than traditional employees.
B. The financial comparison changes when benefits are included in calculations.
C. The ILO opposes all forms of gig work.
D. Traditional employment offers no advantages over gig work.
ANSWER
정답: B "when benefits are factored in" = 복리후생을 포함하면 → 포함 여부에 따라 비교 결과가 달라진다 infer 문제는 지문에서 직접 말하지 않았지만 논리적으로 도출 가능한 것을 고르기
EX-20 HARD
다음 지문을 읽고 물음에 답하세요.
Artificial intelligence is reshaping the consulting industry. Major firms now use AI tools for data analysis, market research, and even preliminary strategy recommendations. Junior consultants, once tasked with these responsibilities, are being redeployed to client-facing roles requiring interpersonal skills. Industry experts predict that within five years, AI will handle up to 40% of routine analytical tasks currently performed by human consultants.
Q. According to the passage, how is AI affecting junior consultants' roles?
A. Junior consultants are being replaced entirely by AI.
B. They are shifting from analytical work to roles requiring human interaction.
C. AI has made junior consultant positions more analytical than before.
D. Junior consultants now focus exclusively on AI development.
ANSWER
정답: B "redeployed to client-facing roles requiring interpersonal skills" = 분석→대인 역할 전환 A의 "entirely"는 과도한 표현, C는 반대 방향
